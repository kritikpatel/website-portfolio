About Me

Kritik Patel (KP) is a 21 year old up and coming independent rapper from the UK. Known for his relatable lyrics on heartbreak and despair, he looks up to artists such as Juice WRLD, Iann Dior, The Kid Laroi and Midwxst to express his experiences and feelings in a unique and catchy way. From sad emo rap to melodic drill, KP is always looking to branch out to evolve and perfect his craft in the music industry.

Listen To My Music:

Latest Release

This single came about after the artist has had multiple experiences with failed relationships and he is expressing how it doesn't always go the way you want it to go, especially when it comes to unrequited love. "I Can't Lie" is an example of how sometimes you can be your complete self but still feel like that isn't enough for the other person. KP conveys this message very emotionally in this song by the relatable lyrics used to draw the listeners in and find out more about the artist.
